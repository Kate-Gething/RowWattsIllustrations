Rowena Watts is a successful Children’s Book Illustrator living in South Oxfordshire. Rowena takes inspiration for her Art from the rural countryside and young family life. Working in watercolor and Indian ink Rowena’s Illustrations capture personality and character. She participates in Artweeks, fairs and exhibitions across the Home Counties. Rowena is happy to take personal commissions to produce unique drawings for self publishing Authors, Children’s bedroom artwork or family pets.
